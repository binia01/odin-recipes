Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Description

Avocado toast with poached eggs is a quick and nutritious breakfast that is perfect for starting your day. Creamy avocado spread over toasted bread, topped with a perfectly poached egg, makes for a delicious combination. It's packed with protein, healthy fats, and fiber, giving you the energy you need to take on the day.

This versatile breakfast can be customized with additional toppings such as tomatoes, radishes, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread, toasted
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (for poaching)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Bring a small pot of water to a gentle simmer. Add vinegar to the water.
  2. Crack each egg into a small bowl, then gently slip it into the simmering water. Poach the eggs for 3-4 minutes, or until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  3. While the eggs are poaching, mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Place one poached egg on top of each slice of avocado toast. Season with additional salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley, and enjoy!
